## C.1

* **a.** $(-3) \div (-2)$ est **positive** (signes identiques)
* **b.** $2 \div (-1)$ est **négative** (signes contraires)
* **c.** $(-3) \div 5$ est **négative** (signes contraires)
* **d.** $\dfrac{-5}{-8}$ est **positive** (signes identiques)
* **e.** $\dfrac{-3}{5}$ est **négative** (signes contraires)
* **f.** $\dfrac{5}{-7}$ est **négative** (signes contraires)

## C.2

* **a.** 
$$\begin{aligned}
\frac{-6}{2} &= \frac{-6 \div 2}{2 \div 2} \\[1em]
&= \frac{-3}{1} \\[1em]
&= -3
\end{aligned}$$

* **b.** 
$$\begin{aligned}
\dfrac{15}{6} &= \dfrac{15 \div 3}{6 \div 3} \\[1em]
&= \dfrac{5}{2}
\end{aligned}$$

* **c.** 
$$\begin{aligned}
\dfrac{-4}{-16} &= \dfrac{4}{16} = \dfrac{4 \div 4}{16 \div 4} \\[1em]
&= \dfrac{1}{4}
\end{aligned}$$

* **d.** 
$$\begin{aligned}
\dfrac{21}{-14} &= \dfrac{-21}{14} \\[1em]
&= \dfrac{-3}{2}
\end{aligned}$$

* **e.**
$$\begin{aligned}
\dfrac{-3}{5} &= \dfrac{-3}{5} \\[1em]
&= \dfrac{-3}{5}
\end{aligned}$$

* **f.** 
$$\begin{aligned}
\dfrac{-150}{-100} &= \dfrac{150}{100} = \dfrac{150 \div 50}{100 \div 50} \\[1em]
&= \dfrac{3}{2}
\end{aligned}$$

## C.3

\end{aligned}$$

\end{aligned}$$

* **c.**

\end{aligned}$$

## C.4

* **a.** 
$$\begin{aligned}
(3 \times 2 - 5) \div (2 - 2 \times 2) &= \dfrac{6 - 5}{2 - 4} \\
&= \dfrac{1}{-2}
\end{aligned}$$
→ quotient **négatif**

* **b.** 
$$\begin{aligned}
\dfrac{-3 \times (-2) + 4}{5 - 3 \times 2} &= \dfrac{6 + 4}{5 - 6} \\
&= \dfrac{10}{-1}
\end{aligned}$$
→ quotient **négatif**

## C.5

* **a.** 
$$\begin{aligned}
\dfrac{5 \times 2 - 7}{5 - 8} &= \dfrac{10 - 7}{5 - 8} \\
&= \dfrac{3}{-3} = -1
\end{aligned}$$

* **b.** 
$$\begin{aligned}
\dfrac{3 \times (-2) + 4}{3 - 3 \times 3} &= \dfrac{-6 + 4}{3 - 9} \\
&= \dfrac{-2}{-6} = \dfrac{1}{3}
\end{aligned}$$

* **c.** 
$$\begin{aligned}
\dfrac{5 - 2 \times 3}{5 \times 6} &= \dfrac{5 - 6}{30} \\
&= \dfrac{-1}{30}
\end{aligned}$$

* **d.** 
$$\begin{aligned}
\dfrac{5 - 36 \div 6}{60 - 8 \times 7} &= \dfrac{5 - 6}{60 - 56} \\
&= \dfrac{-1}{4}
\end{aligned}$$

## C.6

* **a.** 
$$\begin{aligned}
\dfrac{2 - [5 - 3 \times (2 - 4)]}{2 - 15 \div 5} &= \dfrac{2 - [5 - 3 \times (-2)]}{2 - 3} \\
&= \dfrac{2 - (5 + 6)}{-1} = \dfrac{2 - 11}{-1} = \dfrac{-9}{-1} = 9
\end{aligned}$$

* **b.**
