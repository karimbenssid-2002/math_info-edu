## C.4

* **a.** 
$$\begin{aligned}
\frac{14}{42} &= \frac{14 \div 2}{42 \div 2} = \frac{7}{21} \\[0.8em]
&= \frac{7 \div 7}{21 \div 7} \\[0.8em]
&= \frac{1}{3}
\end{aligned}$$

* **b.** 
$$\begin{aligned}
\frac{120}{180} &= \frac{120 \div 10}{180 \div 10} = \frac{12}{18} \\[0.8em]
&= \frac{12 \div 6}{18 \div 6} \\[0.8em]
&= \frac{2}{3}
\end{aligned}$$

* **c.** 
$$\begin{aligned}
\frac{18}{42} &= \frac{18 \div 2}{42 \div 2} = \frac{9}{21} \\[0.8em]
&= \frac{9 \div 3}{21 \div 3} \\[0.8em]
&= \frac{3}{7}
\end{aligned}$$

* **d.** 
$$\begin{aligned}
\frac{224}{84} &= \frac{224 \div 2}{84 \div 2} = \frac{112}{42} \\[0.8em]
&= \frac{112 \div 2}{42 \div 2} = \frac{56}{21} \\[0.8em]
&= \frac{56 \div 7}{21 \div 7} \\[0.8em]
&= \frac{8}{3}
\end{aligned}$$
